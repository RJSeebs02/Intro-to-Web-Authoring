Basic Pasta

Ingredients

  • Dried Pasta (Any pasta can work well)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato Puree
  • Cheese/Tuna/Both/None

Methods

  1. Boil your pasta adding salt to the water later on.
  2. While pasta is cooking grate your cheese/drain your tuna.
  3. When the pasta is cooked add about half a tube of tomato puree, stirring well, to give you a passata a consistency. Of course you can just use passata if you want, and drain the pasta instead.
  4. Then, add the tuna if using any, stirring it in well. Leave for a minute to heat up the tuna. Add salt/pepper to taste.
  5. Serve into a bowl and sprinkle lots of cheese on top.

Enjoy! This can be enjoyed as a simple tomato pasta, or cheese or tuna, or both. :)
